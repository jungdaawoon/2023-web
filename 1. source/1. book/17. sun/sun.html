Publication ‘1 to A’

Collaboration project with Jusun, he is an contemporary Artist. His artistic work mainly deals with things that have been banned in our society for a long time not even himself could know. He explores it with a queer perspective and excavates images. Through various media such as sculpture, installation, painting and Performance. The essence of his work extends the boundaries of art and looks up a deeper level of communication beyond personal experience.

Texts in this publication are fragments of his thoughts written in Korean. English texts in used are ‘Psychology of the Unconscious’ by C. G. Jung

Printing, Editorial, Illustration, Photography

Clients: Self-initiated
