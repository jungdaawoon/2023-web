Publication ‘Breathing Universe’ design

Textile designer also stylist Hyewon Jeong’s thesis. This study aims to explore how an artist creates his Universe and visualizes this by making objects. To discover how significant embodiment is, both as an action and for artists themselves. What does Universe mean for artists? And how does it be expressed and through the medium? Furthermore, referring to iconography as theoretical research, it will be explained by examining graduation project “Breathing universe”

Publication, Editorial, Typography, Printing

Clients: Hyewon Jeong
