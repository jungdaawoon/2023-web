Housing Problems Campaign

The housing problem in The Netherlands is related not only international also local dutch students. They have the same issue getting a house. So The Idea is that expressing both groups student's difficulties.

The image of the house seems a foggy, illusion, or fake. Below there is an image of the house's floor plan. There is written in dutch 'Even if you can read this, it doesn't mean you can get a house' and it is repeated in English in the middle.

This sentence has multiple meanings like

1. even if you can read Dutch.
2. even if you know how to read the floor plan of the house.
3. even if you can access information about the house.

Anyway, you will not get a house.
