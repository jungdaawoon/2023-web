Poster for the music festival 'Hail'.

All of the musicians are new singers or bands and they are trying to do their originality. The title 'Hail'(tsunami) was visually expressed to show their desire to create a new flow. The new flow always accompanies the unknown, so the error mark is placed next to the names.
