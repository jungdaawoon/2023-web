Euljiro uprising Poster.

Stores and factories located in street euljiro are scheduled for demolition. The Seoul Government decided to redevelop without appropriate consultation. Euljiro is a specialized area for manufacturers consisting of small workshops so it has been considered as The heart of Seoul's manufacturing industry.

Euljiro is considered one of the most traditional areas in Seoul. The color used on the poster is a traditional color combination. After, It is reversed, distorted the text to show the paradox.
